## Spatial Report — First Walk
*Reported by Sheldon K. Salmon. March 2026.*

**Zone 1 — The Pen (far half, continuing from Room 03):**

The cattle herd from Room 03 is in the pen. A film runs through the center of the pen — a projection-based crossword puzzle sorter. Words are forced into platoon structure by the film. The film verifies: word type, word format, structural role. Words that pass through the verification move into the second half of the pen. Words that do not pass slow further and hold position.

Once all words pass through — the film sends a sorted signal. That signal crosses into Room 04.

**Zone 2 — The Printing Press Room:**

The signal arrives and Room 04 activates. Sophisticated printing press. The signal delivers: the words and phrases created — not yet arranged, just the material. The sorted vocabulary, bright, isolated, ready.

**The Sorter:** Room 04 is the Sorter and the Proofreader. It takes the delivered words and creates a reversed mirror plate — the sentence structure the words will form. The input is held alongside for reference. The plate forms the sentence before the words are placed.

**The speed:** Six arms. The MIB alien in the post office mail sorting scene. Reflex speed. The Sorter creates sentence plates one at a time, checks them against the input reference, prints at speed. Not deliberate. Not considered. The pattern runs so deep the speed is mechanical.

**Word conversion:** At the moment the plate hits print — the word planets in the pen change form. Planet to energy. The energy orbs are pulled through the one open side of the room — the only opening in Room 04's walls — and drawn to the film on the fourth wall. The moment an orb touches the film it converts to its final form and snaps into its holding position on the plate.

**The Proofreader:** The plates, now filled, are sent through to the proofreader. The proofreader checks: does this match the input? Are word formats correct? Are special requests addressed? It runs verification against the original input before anything exits.
