## Spatial Report — First Walk
*Reported by Sheldon K. Salmon. March 2026.*

**Entry atmosphere:** Twilight zone aesthetic — not the original. The beautiful version. Timeless. Open. More modern, more alive. The only ground is a red carpet extending forward. No walls. No ceiling. Space itself surrounds the path — stars, galaxies, depth without boundary in every direction.

**Structure:** A dual-door crystal elevator at the end of the red carpet. Crystal clarity — you can see the mechanism before it opens. It does not hide what it is.

**On entry:** All the words follow you in. Every candidate. The elevator becomes cramped. The entire selection field presses in around you — not a displayed menu but a physical compression. The candidates are present, bodies in a space that was not designed for all of them at once.

**The door closes.**

**The reflex:** A break of a second — almost involuntary, like a reflex. Not a decision. The button on the pole — 3ft off the ground — shifts from red to green on its own. The system recognizes readiness. The signal fires because conditions are met, not because anyone decided.

**The other side opens and pulls:** The far door opens and draws everything out — not a step but a float. You and the words drift into the bullish-colored space environment beyond. Stars. Galaxies. Below, the word planets are visible — high-density planets glowing distinct from the low-density mass.

**The saffron effect:** The high-density planets activate. They rise from their nested positions and begin moving toward the wormhole — not waiting to be chosen. They call. A saffron on a school trip, recognizing its group, moving toward them. Selection is mutual: the word moves toward you while you move toward the word. Two bodies finding each other in a gravitational field.

**The LAV and ECF gate:**
Because the cleaning happened before entry — LAV root-defensibility scan, ECF density filter — the pull is toward the high-density cluster. The low-density planets are present in the field but their gravity does not reach the cleaned candidates. They do not call. They do not rise. The gate was upstream. The field simply reflects what it received.
