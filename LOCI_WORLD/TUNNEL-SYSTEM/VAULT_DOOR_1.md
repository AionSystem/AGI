## Specification

**Location:** Back wall of the Lobby. Centered. The wall that had nothing before March 2026.

**Material:** Obsidian. Black, dense, zero surface reflection.

**Glow:** Blue-red ambient light emanating from the seams between the obsidian and the wall frame. Not the door itself — the cracks. Blue dominant. Red underneath. They do not mix. They coexist at the boundary.

**Scale:** Larger than the five numbered doors. This is a tunnel door, not a room door.

**Mechanism:** No visible handle. Opens inward on approach. No key. No code. Proximity opens it.

**What is beyond it:** The slope. Zone 1. Main Hall.
