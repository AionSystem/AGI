**Object:** The Red Door
**Type:** Entry mechanism + emergency exit dual function
**Location:** Main entry point into the loci world

**Function 1 — Entry:**
Standard entrance into the loci. Not always required — entry can be direct. The door is the conscious decision to go in.

**Function 2 — Emergency Exit:**
Upon entry, door converts to a red ball of light, tennis-ball size. Follows Sheldon throughout the loci. Can be activated at any point to exit the space immediately.

**Portable:**
The Red Door orb travels throughout the entire loci and all sub-dimensions including the Snow Globe worlds and the Secret Workshop.
