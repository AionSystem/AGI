**Layer:** 3 — Outer Ring
**Contents:** Dense fog extending to dome edges.

**Function:**
Cross-domain idea retrieval. When stuck on a concept — enter the fog and search adjacent domains for components that fit. Not the whole piece — just a component. Assembles novel structures from fragments across domains.

**Primary source material:**
Cartoons and anime — imaginative and abstract enough to yield real structural components for framework building.

**Process:**
Stand in fog. Pull imagery, not words. Emergence by spatial vision, not verbal reasoning. Components surface, are assessed, assembled into the needed structure.
