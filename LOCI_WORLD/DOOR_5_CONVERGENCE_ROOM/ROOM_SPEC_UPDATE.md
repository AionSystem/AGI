## The Back Room — Added March 2026

**Discovery:** March 2026 session. A doorway formed beside the Visual Screen during architectural exploration. The back room was found, not built.

**Dimensions:** 10 x 20  
**Entry:** Door beside the Visual Screen on the back wall  
**Table:** White. Rises from the floor at room center.  
**Model:** 3D holographic emergence architecture. Floats above the table. Navigable from all angles.

**What the back room holds:**
The same emergence process shown on the wall Screen — but in three dimensions. Standing in the back room, the full architecture is visible from all angles, including the input approach and the output departure that the flat Screen cannot show.

**What the 3D model revealed:**
Standing in the back room, two categories of missing architecture became visible that the flat Screen could not show:
- Input side: LAV gate and ECF tagger fire before the word planet field — not captured in the original 3-section model
- Output side: LAV Q&A, ECF Q&A, and SIEVE-OUT were absent — three functions missing from the exit layer

**Relationship to the wall Screen:**
The wall Screen remains accurate for what it shows. The back room extends it. The 2D projection on the wall is the simplified view. The 3D model in the back room is the full architecture. Neither replaces the other.

**Current state of the 3D model:**
The model shows the 5-layer corrected architecture — SIEVE-IN, LAV Gate + ECF Tagger, Word Planet Field + Wormhole + Sorting, LAV Q&A + ECF Q&A, SIEVE-OUT. UNMAPPED zones appear as fog in the model — visible as territory, not yet walkable.
