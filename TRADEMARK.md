## Common Law Trademark Rights

The marks listed above are protected under common law trademark rights established through continuous use in commerce and publication beginning no later than 2025. Common law trademark protection arises automatically upon use — formal registration is not required for protection to exist.

Common law rights are enforceable against:
- Confusingly similar marks used in the same or related fields
- Marks that would mislead consumers or researchers about the source of work
- Use of these marks in commercial products or services without authorization
